Welcome to Houston!


Houston also called H-Town is the fifth largest city in the US. Contained in the massive city are many different professional sports teams.

  • Texans (NFL)
  • Rockets (NBA)
  • Astros (MLB)
  • Dynamo (MLS)
  • Dash (NWSL)

Houston is the birth home of some very notable celabrities such as Beyonce, Hillary Duff, Megan Thee Stalion and Jennifer Garner just to name a few. With a large diverse population this costal city is truely a place all it's own. Residing just of the Gulf of Mexico Houston is major tourist attractionduring the summer months. In addition Houston is home to the NASA space center and museum. Annually the Houston Livestock and Rodeo occures in early March.
