Welcome to Dallas!

A champion is simply someone who did not give up when he wanted to. - Tom Landry. This Quote from former Dallas Cowboys head coach Tom Landry defines Dallas as a city. Dallas is located next to Fort Worth Texas. Between these two sister cities the population of the Dallas-Forth Worth metroplalitan area is nearly three million! Dallas may be synonymous with the Cowboys but it is home to many major leauge teams.

  • Cowboys (NFL)
  • Mavericks (NBA)
  • Rangers (MLB)
  • FC Dallas (MLS)
  • Wings (WNBA)
  • Stars (NHL)

Dallas is home to the annual Texas State Fair. The Fair aligns with the Cotton Bowl played between the University of Texas Longhorns and the University of Oklahoma Sooners. This NCAA Football rivalry takes place in Dallas the hafway point between the two schools. Dallas has many attractions including the Reunion Tower, George W. Bush Presidental Center, Six Flags Over Texas, and the JFK Memorial. Celebrities from Dallas include Owen Wilson, Usher, Demi Lovato, and Vanilla Ice just to name a few.
